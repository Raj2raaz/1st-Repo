Project "AgileWorkScape":
Meaning: "Agile" signifies the project's flexibility and ability to adapt to changing work needs. "WorkScape" combines "work" and "landscape," highlighting the diverse and ever-evolving work environments the project aims to create.

Project "WorkWave":
Meaning: Just as a wave is flexible and constantly in motion, this name symbolizes the fluidity and dynamic nature of the new work arrangements being introduced.
